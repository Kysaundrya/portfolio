Selena Quintanilla was born on April 16, 1971 and was killed March 31, 1995. She was two weeks from her 24th birthday. she had been married for just under 4 years to Chris Perez at the time of her death.

Selena was born April 16, 1971 in Lake Jackson, Texas. She first started her music a the age of 10 wiht her brother and sister. They released 7 songs specialized in Tejano music. in 1989 she became a solo artist under her brothers lable, Kumbia Kings.
